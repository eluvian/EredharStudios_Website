Heroes of the Seas

Status: Completed

Heroes of the Seas is a fan project crafted with the help of and together with the vibrant Sea of Thieves community. Set forth on an adventure through a quaint port and village and gaze upon a delightful showcase of wooden-framed portraits, each capturing the essence of the beloved pirate characters alongside breathtaking screenshots. As you wander around, encounter plenty of colourful characters who eagerly share amusing anecdotes and tales of daring escapades from fellow players.
