The Forgotten Kingdom

Status: Completed

Adventure through the remnants of a long-lost underground kingdom in this short puzzle game. As you navigate the crumbling halls and mysterious chambers, your wit and cunning will be put to the test. Engage in a unique blend of light-based puzzles, where you must skillfully manipulate beams of light, bouncing them off surfaces to activate ancient mechanisms and unlock hidden pathways. You won't be alone in this adventure. An AI companion will accompany you, lending a helping hand when needed.
